<div class="content-section">
  <h1 class="section-title">Project Charter</h1>
  
  <div class="info-box">
    <h2 class="subsection-title">Project Overview</h2>
    <p>The AI Faculty Support Portal project aims to create a comprehensive solution that integrates AI-powered tools into Silverleaf University's academic workflows, enhancing faculty productivity and student learning experiences.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="info-box">
    <h2 class="subsection-title">Project Objectives</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Develop a syllabus-aligned AI assistant for faculty support</li>
      <li>Implement automated feedback generation for student submissions</li>
      <li>Create a streamlined assignment workflow system</li>
      <li>Ensure secure, compliant AI infrastructure with both cloud and local options</li>
      <li>Maintain ethical standards and transparency in AI usage</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  
  <div class="info-box">
    <h2 class="subsection-title">Project Scope</h2>
    <p>The project encompasses the design, development, and deployment of an AI-powered portal that serves faculty, students, and administrators at Silverleaf University. The solution includes AI integration, dashboard analytics, and comprehensive workflow management.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="info-box">
    <h2 class="subsection-title">Success Criteria</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Successful integration of AI tools with university course content</li>
      <li>Reduction in faculty administrative workload</li>
      <li>Improved feedback turnaround time for students</li>
      <li>Compliance with FERPA, GDPR, and university AI policies</li>
      <li>Positive user feedback from faculty and students</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
