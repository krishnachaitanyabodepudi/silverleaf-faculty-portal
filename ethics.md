# Ethical & Social Considerations

Ethical concerns guided the solution from the start. The system emphasizes:  
- **Bias Mitigation:** Human oversight on all AI outputs  
- **Transparency:** Clear indication of AI involvement  
- **Privacy Protection:** Local LM Studio deployment and no external data sharing  
- **Accessibility:** Inclusive design for diverse needs  
- **Compliance:** FERPA, GDPR, and institutional AI policies
